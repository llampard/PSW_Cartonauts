Around the World in 80 Days

Namaste, enjoy your stay in Nepal.

You've made it to Kathmandu. Stepping into the city, you're greeted with a melting pot of cultures and people. The building looks very ancient and many people are walking the streets. Your first stop is to Budahnilkantha, a Buddhist shrine of the sleeping Vishnu, and pray for your luck and fortune in this journey. Next, you catch a flight around the Himalayan Mountains and capture their intense beauty. From there, you relax in the city and try new local cuisine. While eating you begin to plan where your trip will take you next.

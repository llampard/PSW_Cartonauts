Around the World in 80 Days

Hei, Helsinki is this way.

Your driver takes you to the airport to catch your flight to Helsinki. When you arrive to Finland you can't seem to shake this weird feeling. You catch the city ferry and forgot to grab a map at the airport. You spend all day looking for the Helsinki Cathedral. Instead you find yourself at the Sky Wheel Helsinki to get a bird's eye view of the city to see if you can find your destination. While at the peak, you see Clarke getting into a cab with his bags, headed to the airport. You rush to find a cab yourself but you're unfamilar with the Finnish language an the people aren't much help. Frustrated and tired you decide to stay in Helsinki for the night. You've lost the game.

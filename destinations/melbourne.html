Around the World in 80 Days

G'day! Let's see what Melbourne is all about.

You've made it to Melbourne, Australia but you don't see Clarke. You assume he took the flight to Iceland. When you step off your plane the flight attendant hands you a note that tells your next clue would be found in Luna Park and there's a car waiting for you outside. You get to the park and a woman is outside the entrance holding a poster with your name on it. You go over to her and she welcomes you to Melbourne and hands you a fast pass to all of the rides. After a long and exciting day, she asks where you'd like to go next.

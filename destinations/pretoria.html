Sawubona, this is Pretoria, South Africa!

A woman greets you at the airport and presents you with your challenge in Pretoria. You have to travel by hot air balloon across safari plains to find the next envelope. From the air, you spot rhinos and lions and wildebeests from above. They're so small they almost look like toys! Once you land, you notice some giraffes having lunch. But then, it hits you that one of the leaves doesn't look like a real leaf. It's your envelope! You have your tickets, but as soon as you take off, the balloon gets popped by a tree branch and you are stuck! End to end, this stop in Pretoria actually lasted 72 days. You've lost the game.

Around the World in 80 Days

Konnichiwa, tour the city of Osaka!

You decided to go to Osaka. With only 15 days left, you pray that Clarke didn't beat you. When you touch down in Japan, you instantly realize the size of this city. There's nearlt 2.7 million people living in Osaka alone! You take the subway to the Osaka Castle where you're greeted by a tour guide and given a small envelope. There you find your final ticket! All you need to do is head to San Franscisco, USA, where you started your race.

Around the World in 80 Days

Jambo! Explore Mombasa, Kenya.

You get to Boi International Airport. You received instructions earlier to go to Diani Beach. You get there and you find a private table with a note, fresh fruit, and orange juice waiting for you. The note attached says "Enjoy your stay! In 2 hours you'll be taken to the Arabuko Sokoke National Reserve." When your car arrives, you head over to the reserve. There you find tons of animals roaming free in their diverse habitats, your guide even lets you take photos with a few animals. You've enjoyed your stay in Kenya and can't wait to visit again after the competition. Where would you like to travel to next?

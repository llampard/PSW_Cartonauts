Around the World in 80 Days

Marhaba, Tunis is right here.

You step into the city of Tunis and you're greeted with a collection of smells from the Medina market. While looking at souqs (hats), you notice a sign about the Bardo Museum, so you decide to try it out. The museum highlights and celebrates ancient North African life by showcasing artwork and artifacts dating from 1228-1547 A.D. After visiting the museum you decide, on where you want to go next.
